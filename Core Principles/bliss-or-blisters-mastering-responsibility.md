# Bliss Or Blisters: Mastering Responsibility

## Definition

Responsibility means I have the ability to choose how I respond.

Responsibility is not fault.

Fault and responsibility are not the same thing.

They are not connected.

Something may not be my fault.

And it is still my responsibility.

Because I still have the ability to choose how I respond.

That is where my power is.

---

## Events Have No Meaning Until We Give Them Meaning

Everything we encounter in life is an event.

Events by themselves do not have real meaning.

We give meaning to the events of our lives.

Two people can experience the same event and have completely different responses.

One person can call it devastating.

Another person can call it freedom.

Same event.

Different perception.

Different meaning.

Different response.

Different result.

---

## Group A And Group B

A person can be in a relationship for twenty years, and one day their partner says they are leaving.

Group A says:

My world has come to an end.

They get angry.

They get hurt.

They say, “How could they do this to me?”

They tell the story over and over.

They gather people around them who agree with their pain.

They justify their suffering.

They go below the line.

Group B has the same event happen and says:

Freedom.

Yahoo.

Party, party, party.

Same event.

Different perception.

One creates blisters.

One creates bliss.

---

## Below The Line

Below the line is where most people live.

Excuses.

Blame.

Justification.

Below the line is disempowering.

Below the line takes power away.

Below the line keeps people stuck in the story.

Below the line keeps people reacting to life instead of creating life.

Excuses are below the line.

Blame is below the line.

Justification is below the line.

There are no rewards below the line.

---

## Above The Line

Above the line is responsibility.

Above the line is where the rewards are.

As soon as I take responsibility for my life, I instantly empower myself.

And with more power, my light gets brighter.

And the brighter my light gets, the more life is attracted to it.

Responsibility is above the line.

Results are above the line.

Power is above the line.

Creation is above the line.

---

## Responsibility Is Response-Ability

Responsibility means I have the ability to choose how I respond.

To respond is to act.

When I take different actions, I get different results.

That is responsibility.

Not blame.

Not fault.

Not shame.

Responsibility.

The ability to choose my response.

---

## The Car Accident

Imagine I am driving through town.

I have the green light.

I drive through the intersection.

Someone else runs a red light and hits my car.

Is that my responsibility?

Yes.

Is it my fault?

No.

Fault and responsibility are not the same thing.

I chose to drive that day.

I chose to be on that street at that time.

I could have left five minutes earlier.

I could have chosen another street.

And even if none of that were true, I still have the ability to choose how I respond to what happens next.

That is responsibility.

---

## Reactive Or Proactive

When I react, I give my power away.

When I react, I make something outside of myself the cause.

Then my life becomes the effect.

That is disempowering.

Reactive is disempowering.

Proactive is empowering.

When I am proactive, I choose how I want to respond.

I become the cause.

I create the effect.

That is personal power.

---

## Cause And Effect

Every cause creates an effect.

Every effect is created by a cause.

When I react to someone else, I am making them the cause of how I feel.

When I make them the cause, I become the effect.

That is powerless.

I cannot control everything that happens outside of me.

I cannot control every person.

I cannot control every event.

But I can take responsibility for how I respond.

That is where my power is.

---

## Bliss Or Blisters

If I take responsibility, I create bliss.

If I blame, justify, and make excuses, I create blisters.

Bliss comes from choosing my response.

Blisters come from reacting.

Bliss is above the line.

Blisters are below the line.

The choice is mine.

---

## Proactive Equals Empowering

Proactive = Empowering

Reactive = Disempowering

When I am proactive, I gain power.

When I am reactive, I lose power.

When I am proactive, I create.

When I am reactive, I complain.

When I am proactive, I respond.

When I am reactive, I blame.

The question is simple:

Where am I choosing to live?

Above the line?

Or below the line?

---

## The Mastery

Mastering responsibility means I stop asking who is at fault.

I start asking:

How am I going to respond?

What action can I take?

How can I make this better?

How would Love respond?

That is responsibility.

That is empowerment.

That is above the line.

That is how I turn blisters into bliss.
