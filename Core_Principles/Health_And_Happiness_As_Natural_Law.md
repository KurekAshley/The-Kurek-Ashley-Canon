# Health And Happiness As Natural Law

## Foundational Doctrine of the Kurek Ashley Canon

---

# Definition

Most people have been taught that health and happiness are rewards.

Rewards for working hard.

Rewards for achieving success.

Rewards for getting life right.

Rewards for becoming enough.

I do not believe that.

I believe health and happiness are our natural state.

They are not rewards.

They are not prizes.

They are not destinations.

They are natural expressions of a life lived in alignment.

Simply stated:

> Health and happiness are natural law.

When we live in harmony with who we truly are, health and happiness naturally emerge.

When we move away from that harmony, suffering increases.

The law has not changed.

We have simply moved out of alignment with it.

---

# Nature Shows Us The Truth

Nature continually reveals this principle.

A tree does not struggle to become a tree.

A flower does not struggle to become a flower.

A bird does not attend seminars to learn how to fly.

Life naturally moves toward expression.

Life naturally moves toward growth.

Life naturally moves toward fulfillment of its potential.

Human beings are part of nature.

Yet many people have forgotten this.

Instead of living naturally, they live in conflict.

Conflict with themselves.

Conflict with their purpose.

Conflict with their values.

Conflict with reality.

The result is often stress, suffering, and disconnection.

---

# We Are Designed For Wellbeing

Look at a child.

A healthy child naturally laughs.

Naturally plays.

Naturally explores.

Naturally loves.

Naturally expresses joy.

Joy is not taught.

It is revealed.

It is already there.

The natural state of a human being is not chronic stress.

It is not anxiety.

It is not resentment.

It is not hopelessness.

These states arise when something has become misaligned.

Just as pain in the body signals physical imbalance, emotional suffering often signals internal imbalance.

---

# The Difference Between Pain And Suffering

Pain is part of life.

Every human being experiences pain.

Loss.

Disappointment.

Failure.

Heartbreak.

Grief.

Challenge.

Pain is unavoidable.

Suffering is different.

Suffering is often created by resistance.

Resistance to what is.

Resistance to reality.

Resistance to growth.

Resistance to change.

Pain may visit us.

Suffering often moves in when we refuse to listen to what pain is trying to teach.

---

# Happiness Is Not Circumstantial

One of the greatest misunderstandings in modern culture is the belief that happiness comes from circumstances.

People say:

"I'll be happy when..."

When I make more money.

When I find the right relationship.

When I lose the weight.

When I become successful.

When life becomes easier.

The problem is that happiness continually moves into the future.

And the future never arrives.

There will always be another goal.

Another challenge.

Another mountain to climb.

Happiness cannot depend entirely on circumstances because circumstances are always changing.

True happiness comes from our relationship with life.

Not merely from the conditions of life.

---

# Happiness Is An Inside Job

For years people have searched outside themselves for what can only be created within.

No amount of achievement can permanently solve an internal disconnection.

No amount of money can create self-worth.

No amount of recognition can create self-love.

No amount of success can create inner peace.

Those qualities must be cultivated from within.

The outer world may support them.

But it cannot create them.

---

# The Frequency Of Health And Happiness

As we teach throughout this Canon:

> Thought creates frequency.

> Frequency creates form.

Health and happiness are influenced by the frequencies we consistently live in.

Love.

Gratitude.

Purpose.

Connection.

Contribution.

Peace.

Compassion.

These frequencies tend to expand life.

Fear.

Resentment.

Victimhood.

Hopelessness.

Bitterness.

Chronic judgment.

These frequencies tend to contract life.

This does not mean positive thinking magically solves everything.

It means that the quality of our inner world influences the quality of our outer experience.

---

# Alignment Creates Wellbeing

One of the most important truths I have discovered is this:

People are often unhappy because they are living out of alignment.

They are saying yes when they mean no.

Living someone else's dream.

Ignoring their values.

Suppressing their truth.

Abandoning their purpose.

Choosing comfort over growth.

The deeper the misalignment, the greater the suffering.

The greater the alignment, the greater the wellbeing.

This is why:

> Alignment creates expansion.

Health and happiness are natural byproducts of alignment.

---

# Purpose, Love, And Happiness

Purpose and happiness are inseparable.

People often believe happiness comes from pleasure.

Pleasure matters.

But pleasure alone rarely creates fulfillment.

Purpose creates meaning.

Meaning creates fulfillment.

Fulfillment creates a deeper form of happiness.

Likewise, love deepens happiness.

Not romantic love alone.

Love for life.

Love for people.

Love for growth.

Love for contribution.

Love expands us.

And what expands us tends to move us closer to wellbeing.

---

# The Contribution Principle

One of the greatest sources of happiness is contribution.

There is something profoundly fulfilling about helping another human being.

About making a difference.

About serving something larger than yourself.

Many people search endlessly for happiness while focusing exclusively on themselves.

Then they discover that happiness grows when their attention expands beyond themselves.

Contribution creates meaning.

Meaning creates fulfillment.

Fulfillment creates happiness.

---

# Health Beyond The Physical

Health is more than the absence of illness.

True health includes:

- Physical health.
- Mental health.
- Emotional health.
- Spiritual health.
- Relational health.

A person can be financially wealthy and emotionally bankrupt.

Physically fit and spiritually empty.

Professionally successful and deeply unhappy.

True health requires wholeness.

The goal is not perfection.

The goal is integration.

---

# The Law Of Growth

Life is constantly inviting us to grow.

Growth is natural.

Expansion is natural.

Evolution is natural.

This is why I often say:

> In life either you evolve or you dissolve.

Nothing stays the same.

When we resist growth, life often becomes painful.

When we embrace growth, life becomes more expansive.

Growth is not punishment.

Growth is life expressing itself.

---

# Happiness Is A Responsibility

Many people view happiness as a personal preference.

I view it differently.

I believe happiness is both a birthright and a responsibility.

Why?

Because what fills us is what spills from us.

A person filled with anger tends to spread anger.

A person filled with fear tends to spread fear.

A person filled with love tends to spread love.

A person filled with happiness tends to spread happiness.

The quality of our inner life influences the lives of everyone around us.

Therefore cultivating happiness is not selfish.

It is service.

---

# Practical Application

Ask yourself:

1. Where am I out of alignment?
2. What truth am I avoiding?
3. What would bring more harmony into my life?
4. Am I living according to my values?
5. Am I pursuing purpose or merely comfort?
6. What am I grateful for today?
7. How can I contribute more?
8. How would love respond?

Then take one action that moves you closer to alignment.

Small changes in alignment often create profound changes in wellbeing.

# The Transformer Principle

Most people spend their lives trying to achieve happiness.

I believe happiness is not something we achieve.

It is something we allow.

It emerges when we stop fighting ourselves.

When we stop abandoning our truth.

When we align with our values.

When we live our purpose.

When we choose love.

When we continue growing.

Health and happiness are not rewards waiting at the end of the journey.

They are signs that we are walking in harmony with life itself.

Because ultimately:

> Health is natural.

> Happiness is natural.

> Alignment is natural.

> Love is natural.

And when we live according to those truths, life tends to work far better than when we resist them.
