# Attention Creates Energy. Intention Organizes It.

## Definition

Attention creates energy.

Intention organizes it.

These two forces work together in every act of creation.

Attention supplies energy.

Intention directs that energy into form.

Without attention there is no fuel.

Without intention there is no direction.

---

## Attention Creates Energy

Anything we consistently give attention to receives energy.

What we focus on grows.

What we repeatedly think about gains strength.

What we repeatedly reinforce gains momentum.

Attention is the mechanism through which energy is supplied.

---

## Intention Organizes Energy

Energy alone is not enough.

Energy requires direction.

Intention provides that direction.

Intention tells energy what form to move toward.

The more specific the intention, the clearer the result.

---

## Why Specificity Matters

Vague intentions produce vague results.

Specific intentions produce specific outcomes.

The universe responds to information.

The clearer the information, the clearer the form.

This is why goals should be specific.

This is why vision should be detailed.

This is why purpose should be clear.

Specificity organizes energy.

---

## The Creation Process

Attention supplies the energy.

Intention supplies the instructions.

Energy then moves toward expression.

This process operates whether we are aware of it or not.

The difference is that conscious creators participate deliberately.

Unconscious creators participate accidentally.

---

## Practical Application

Ask yourself:

What am I giving my attention to every day?

What am I energizing?

What am I feeding?

Then ask:

What is my intention?

What result am I directing this energy toward?

When attention and intention become aligned, creation accelerates.

---

## Key Distinction

Attention without intention creates scattered results.

Intention without attention creates weak results.

When attention and intention work together, energy becomes organized and moves powerfully toward the desired outcome.
