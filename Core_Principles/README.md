# Core Principles

## The Foundational Doctrines of the Kurek Ashley Canon

The Core Principles represent the foundational laws upon which the entire Kurek Ashley Canon is built.

These principles emerged through decades of coaching, observation, study, teaching, personal experience, and the transformation of thousands of individuals across diverse areas of life.

Every framework, methodology, distinction, strategy, and application found within the Canon can ultimately be traced back to one or more of these principles.

Together they form the foundation of The Unified Theory of Transformation.

---

# Foundational Architecture

The current architecture of transformation can be expressed as:

```text
Thought
    ↓
Frequency
    ↓
Perception
    ↓
Meaning
    ↓
Self-Image
    ↓
Rules
    ↓
Emotions
    ↓
Choices
    ↓
Actions
    ↓
Results
```

Transformation occurs when one or more levels of this architecture are consciously upgraded.
