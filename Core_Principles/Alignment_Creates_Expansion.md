# Alignment Creates Expansion

## Definition

Expansion is the natural result of alignment.

Most people try to create expansion by pushing harder.

Working harder.

Trying harder.

Forcing more.

Controlling more.

But expansion does not come from force.

Expansion comes from alignment.

When every part of our being is moving in the same direction, expansion becomes inevitable.

---

# Alignment Comes Before Expansion

Most people want the expansion first.

They want more money.

More success.

More opportunity.

More influence.

More freedom.

More impact.

But if those things arrive before alignment, the cybernetic mechanism pulls them back to where the self-image says they belong.

That is why people can create success and then lose it.

Create momentum and then sabotage it.

Create opportunity and then shrink.

The issue is not expansion.

The issue is alignment.

---

# Pull Everything Into Alignment

One of the greatest things we can do is pull everything into alignment.

Our values.

Our purpose.

Our self-image.

Our goals.

Our expectations.

Our language.

Our actions.

Our identity.

Everything begins moving toward the same focal point.

It is like lining up antennas.

One by one we align every part of our being to the same frequency.

When everything is aligned to the same frequency, it can only take that form.

---

# Values Create Alignment

This is one of the reasons we do the Values Process.

People often think the Values Process is about discovering values.

It isn't.

It is about alignment.

When we are out of alignment, life feels harder.

When we are in alignment, life feels easier.

Not because life changed.

Because we changed.

The Values Process continually brings us back into alignment with who we really are.

---

# The Truth Of Who We Are

The more we operate in alignment with the truth of who we are, the easier life becomes.

Our brain accepts it.

Our nervous system accepts it.

Our emotions support it.

Our actions become natural.

We stop fighting ourselves.

We stop creating internal conflict.

We stop pulling in opposite directions.

Instead, all of our energy begins moving together.

---

# Alignment Raises Frequency

Alignment raises frequency.

And frequency determines form.

When our values, identity, self-image, goals, expectations, emotions, and actions are aligned, we begin operating on a different frequency.

That frequency starts attracting different opportunities.

Different people.

Different ideas.

Different resources.

Different possibilities.

Not because the universe suddenly changed.

Because we changed frequency.

---

# Expansion Is A Byproduct

Expansion is not the goal.

Alignment is the goal.

Expansion is the byproduct.

When we become aligned with our purpose, expansion occurs.

When we become aligned with our values, expansion occurs.

When we become aligned with our self-image, expansion occurs.

When we become aligned with the truth of who we are, expansion occurs.

Expansion is simply life expressing itself through a clear channel.

---

# The Formula

The more aligned we become,

the more energy flows.

The more energy flows,

the higher our frequency.

The higher our frequency,

the greater our ability to receive.

The greater our ability to receive,

the greater our expansion.

Alignment creates expansion.

Not because expansion is forced.

Because expansion is the natural expression of alignment.
