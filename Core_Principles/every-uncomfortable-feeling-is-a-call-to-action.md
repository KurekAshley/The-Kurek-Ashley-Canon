# Every Uncomfortable Feeling Is A Call To Action

## Definition

Every uncomfortable feeling is a call to action to do something different.

Not a call to suffer.

Not a call to complain.

Not a call to analyze.

A call to action.

A call to do something different.

---

## Your System Is Working Perfectly

Most people think uncomfortable feelings mean something is wrong.

I teach the opposite.

Your system is working perfectly.

The feeling is feedback.

The feeling is information.

The feeling is telling you that what you're currently thinking, focusing on, or doing is producing a frequency that is taking you away from what you want.

The feeling is not the problem.

The feeling is the messenger.

---

## Emotions Are Feedback

Your emotions are your conscious awareness of what frequency you're on.

If you feel empowered, you're on one frequency.

If you feel frustrated, angry, overwhelmed, fearful, or discouraged, you're on another.

The emotion is giving you feedback.

It's telling you where you are.

The question is:

What are you going to do with the information?

---

## Stop Asking Why

One of the biggest mistakes people make is getting caught in:

"Why am I feeling this?"

Then they create more references.

More stories.

More reasons.

More evidence.

And they end up feeling worse.

Instead of asking:

"Why am I feeling this?"

Ask:

"What is this feeling calling me to do differently?"

---

## The Feeling Is A Signal

Every uncomfortable feeling is a signal.

A signal that something needs to change.

Maybe your thinking needs to change.

Maybe your focus needs to change.

Maybe your actions need to change.

Maybe your environment needs to change.

Maybe the people you're spending time with need to change.

Maybe the story you're telling yourself needs to change.

The feeling is simply telling you:

Something different is required.

---

## Focus On The Vision

When the uncomfortable feeling shows up, do not focus on the problem.

Focus on the vision.

Focus on the outcome.

Focus on what you want.

Think about the vision.

Talk about the vision.

Create the vision.

Move toward the vision.

Most people keep talking about the problem.

I already know the problem.

The problem created the feeling.

Now let's use the feeling to create a different result.

---

## Everything Is Trying To Get You What You Want

This is one of the most important distinctions.

People think uncomfortable feelings are against them.

They are not.

They are for them.

Everything is trying to get you what you want.

The feeling is showing you that your current frequency, focus, or actions are not aligned with the outcome.

So it gives you feedback.

The feeling is helping you.

Not hurting you.

---

## Evolution Requires Adjustment

As we grow, we adjust.

As we evolve, we change.

As we learn, we refine.

The uncomfortable feeling is part of that process.

It is guiding us.

It is redirecting us.

It is helping us evolve.

If something no longer feels right, pay attention.

Make an adjustment.

Take a new action.

Move in a new direction.

Keep evolving.

---

## The Choice

When an uncomfortable feeling appears, you have two choices.

You can focus on the feeling.

Or you can use the feeling.

Most people focus on the feeling.

Successful people use the feeling.

They recognize it.

They thank it.

They learn from it.

And then they do something different.

---

## The Mastery

The mastery is simple.

Every uncomfortable feeling is a call to action.

A call to action to do something different.

A call to action to think differently.

A call to action to focus differently.

A call to action to act differently.

A call to action to move closer to what you want.

The feeling is not your enemy.

The feeling is your guide.

Listen to it.

Use it.

Then take action.
